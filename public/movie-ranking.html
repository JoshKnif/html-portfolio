The Best Movies According to Joshua Knifton

Joshua's top 3 movies of all time!

Winners and Sinners

One of my favourite Sammo Hung fils. I would watch this growing up with my grandfather often and we would always have the best laugh!

Finding Nemo

I am a sucker for animation and an even bigger sucker for Australian accents! Finding Nemo incorporates both and has exceptionally great visials. Did you know, that the animation crew did diving to learn how the sun reflects through the water in order to animate it correctly.

The Lion King

You guessed it, another animation! This one I hold dear as I grew up watching this movie on VHS over and over. I would cry each time Mufasa died.
